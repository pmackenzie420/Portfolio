EcoSentinel

This project is creating a smart, autonomous drone that protects crops and property without harming wildlife. Using ArduPilot for flight control and a Raspberry Pi 4 for real-time thermal imaging, the drone detects animals and responds with flashing lights and sound to safely scare them away. The goal is to build a scalable, AI-powered platform that reduces pesticide use, prevents crop loss, and could even help monitor endangered species — a practical, humane, and sustainable approach to pest control.
